WHY GO FOR A IQ TEST??

  • A typical way of determining the relative intelligence of different people, is to give them an IQ test and compare the scores
  • A person's Intelligence Quotient (IQ) is, arguably, one of their most important characteristics.
  • It is believed that you can predict a person's behavior more accurately from their IQ than from any other single piece of information about them
